Ludem Locus

Ludem Locus is my first VR project, developed using Unity and C#. This chess-themed escape room combines my passion for chess with interactive VR gameplay. Players navigate the virtual environment using joystick controls, solving chess-related puzzles as they explore.

While proud of this initial project, I see potential for improvement, especially in the visuals. I plan to refine the environment and graphical effects to enhance immersion and player experience in future iterations.

Soaring Krag

Soaring Krag is a 3D variation of the classic Flappy Bird game, where you control Krag, a flying rock, navigating through a series of obstacles. Unlike the traditional game, the walls are made of "rock, paper, scissors" elements where each wall presents a different type of challenge that corresponds to the game's theme.

This project was developed entirely by me, and it allowed me to experiment with 3D mechanics and player movement. I designed the game's mechanics, environment, and obstacles, and worked on optimizing the game for a fun and engaging experience. It was an excellent opportunity to expand my skills in Unity and game design.
